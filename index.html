Herschel Walker

Herschel Walker played for the University of Georgia from 1980 to 1982. He is one of the best (if not the best) football players to ever come out of UGA. Many would argue he should go down as the best college player ever.

Beginning with carrying the Bulldogs to their first national championship as a FRESHMAN, he continued his dominance for the next two seasons. A dominant athlete with height and bulk never seen before on such a young runningback, he was a man amongst boys. He dominated defenses and gave other teams nightmares. Many experts agree that even in today's age of superior size and speed on these athletes, Herschel Walker would still own the competition. He was simply way ahead of his time, and will go down as the GOAT for the University of Georgia.

He went on to play in the National Football League and do many other things with his life including boxing, which shows how amazing of an athlete he is. But nobody will forget how dominant he was as a bulldog. To this day, dominant runningbacks will always be compared to Walker as the prototype for a total package runningback.
